ESP32-S3 BLE Clicker

A simple BLE remote clicker using the Adafruit ESP32-S3.

09/09/2023

I recently picked up an ESP32-S3 device from Adafruits. It's a compact little thing with a built in 240x135 TFT display, 3 programmable buttons, a battery monitoring chip, NeoPixel LED, and a whole load more.
